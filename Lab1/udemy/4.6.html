My Favorite Artists

Twenty One Pilots

An American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio, known for their genre-blurring style combining pop, rock, hip-hop, and electronic music.

  • Famous songs: "Stressed Out", "Ride", "Heathens"
  • Awards: Grammy for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance
  • Key members: Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun

Linkin Park

A legendary rock band blending nu-metal, alternative rock, and electronic elements, with iconic albums like *Hybrid Theory* and *Meteora*.

  • Famous songs: "In the End", "Numb", "Crawling"
  • Awards: Multiple Grammy and MTV awards
  • Key members: Chester Bennington, Mike Shinoda

BTS

A South Korean boy band that has taken the world by storm with their powerful performances and meaningful lyrics.

  • Famous songs: "Dynamite", "Butter", "Fake Love"
  • Awards: Billboard Music Awards, AMAs, and more
  • Key members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, Jungkook

SEVENTEEN

A South Korean boy group known for their impressive self-production skills, incredible choreography, and versatile music.

  • Famous songs: "Don't Wanna Cry", "Left & Right", "HOT"
  • Awards: Mnet Asian Music Awards, Golden Disc Awards
  • Key members: S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, and more
